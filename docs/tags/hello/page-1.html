Team

We are a group of 4 tech savvy Elixir freaks that want to share their experience and encourage people to expand their knowledge of coding.

Adam Skołuda

Developer

I am a cheerful guy who really enjoys programming, speaking and extending knowledge. My colleagues would describe me as a person who asks many questions, maybe sometimes too many. :) I like working in the office, talking to people helping them and learning from them. This is the reason why I organize Elixir workshops. In my free time, I'm developing some private projects, playing football in amateur leagues, playing board games, dancing, socializing. I've been working as a professional developer for 6 years in various technologies, mainly RoR and Elixir.
